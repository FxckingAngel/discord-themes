# Silverfox0338

I'm someone who's passionate about customization, if something can be tweaked, restyled, or made to feel more *mine*, I'm going to do it. That obsession bleeds into a lot of things, and CSS just happens to be one of the best outlets for it. There's something satisfying about taking a UI you stare at every day and making it actually feel like yours.

I own and maintain this repo, and honestly? I'd love nothing more than to see it grow. If you've got a theme you're proud of, whether it's polished or a work in progress (still usable ofc), this is a place for it. I'm very open to contributors of all skill levels, so don't be shy.
